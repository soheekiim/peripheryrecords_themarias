THE MARIAS

The Marías is an indie band based out of Los Angeles, California, that plays psychedelic soul. The band is composed of Puerto Rican lead singer María Zardoya and Los Angeles native and drummer Josh Conway. Their music infuses elements of jazz percussion, guitar riffs, smoke-velvet vocals, and horn solos. The Marías have been active seen 2016.
